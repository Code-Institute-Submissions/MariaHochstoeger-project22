How to play

  • Upon clicking "Let's start!" the game begins and you will see the first question.
  • Upon choosing your answer, you will receive feedback whether your answer is correct or not.
  • Click "Next" to get to the next question.
  • After all questions have been answered, you will see your score.
  • If you wish to play again, you can do so by clicking "Play again".
